Vision

When I decided to enroll in 2020, I imagined a place where students create the technology that other students use. It saddens me that most systems in UCT were not built by students, it makes sense that we learn so we can build products for our community.

Above all, the goal and vision of this project is to inspire students in every faculty to be at the forefront of building systems for our community rather than leaving it to the institution.

So I plead and beg each and everyone of you.This is our community. The systems we use, such as Payandconnect, were built by people like us. It is our responsibility to build systems for the next generation of UCT students.

Whether it's selling make-up products, cupcakes, or any other endeavor. Together we can make an impact. No matter what year you are in, you can make a change and build something for the UCT community.
We love you ❤️

~Tshepo Mkhululi Ncube (Co-Founder & Student)
